## ðŸ“‹ The Bidding Process

### Step 1: Review the Opportunity

When you find an interesting opportunity, review:

**ðŸ“„ Basic Information**
- Title and opportunity number
- Closing date and time
- Estimated value
- Type (RFP, RFQ, ITT, etc.)

**ðŸ“‘ Documents**
- Download ALL attachments
- Read terms and conditions
- Review specifications
- Check for mandatory requirements

**ðŸ“ Special Requirements**
- Mandatory site visit?
- Pre-qualification needed?
- Bonding requirements?
- Insurance minimums?

**ðŸ’¡ Multiple Lots?**
- Can you bid on just some parts?
- Lot-specific requirements
- Separate pricing per lot

---

### Step 2: Ask Questions

Have questions? You can:

**Ask via BC Bid Portal**
- Submit through the opportunity page
- Questions are anonymous
- All suppliers see answers

**Ask via Email**
- Email the contact listed
- Get direct response
- May be published as addendum

**Viewing Answers**
- Check the "Questions & Answers" section
- Watch for addenda (official answers)
- You MUST acknowledge all addenda

> âš ï¸ **Important**: Questions have deadlines! Usually 7-10 days before closing.

---

### Step 3: Prepare Your Bid

**Two Types of Submissions:**

#### **Standard Process (RFP, RFQ)**

1. **Review opportunity info** - Re-read everything
2. **Manage your team** - Assign team members to help
3. **Check planholders list** - See who else is bidding
4. **Complete forms** - Fill out all required sections
5. **Upload documents** - Attach certifications, references, examples
6. **Submit** - Before the deadline!

#### **Simplified Process (ITQ, Timber Auction, ITT)**

1. **Opportunity Information** - Review details
2. **Process Rules** - Understand specific requirements
3. **Responses Tab** - Answer questions/questionnaire
4. **Pricing Tab** - Enter your pricing
   - Unit prices
   - Line items
   - Total amount
5. **Submit** - Get confirmation number

---

### Step 4: Monitor for Changes

**Amendments** = Changes to the opportunity
- Extended deadline
- Scope changes
- Modified requirements

**Addenda** = Additional information
- Q&A responses
- Clarifications
- Document corrections

**âš ï¸ CRITICAL**: You MUST acknowledge all amendments and addenda
- Check daily until closing
- Failure to acknowledge can disqualify your bid

---

### Step 5: Final Submission Options

**Create Multiple Submissions**
- Submit alternate approaches
- Different pricing scenarios
- Various solutions

**Copy a Submission**
- Duplicate your response
- Modify for alternate version
- Saves time on similar bids

**Withdraw if Needed**
- You can withdraw before closing
- Cannot withdraw after deadline
- Formal withdrawal process

**Cancel Draft**
- Delete in-progress submissions
- No record kept
- Start fresh if needed
