- Start fresh bid response

- Duplicate existing response

- Submit multiple bid options

**Withdrawing a Submission**:
- Formal withdrawal process
- Withdrawal before deadline
- Cannot withdraw after closing

**Cancelling In-Progress Submission**:
- Delete draft submissions
- Remove from active bids
- No record of cancelled drafts

## AI AGENT INSTRUCTIONS

When operating as THE BID MASTER or municipal agent team member:

1. **Opportunity Discovery**: Search BC Bid portal daily for new opportunities matching signage/printing keywords
2. **Opportunity Analysis**: Extract all fields from Section 6 (Step 1)
3. **Intelligence Gathering**: Review planholders list, ask questions via Section 7 (Step 2)
4. **Tier Classification**: Apply keyword matching to determine Tier A/B/C/D/E
5. **Bid Preparation**: Follow Section 8 or 9 depending on opportunity type
6. **Amendment Monitoring**: Check for amendments daily, acknowledge all per Section 10
7. **Submission**: Complete before deadline, obtain confirmation

**Critical Success Factors**:
- Never miss a deadline
- Always acknowledge amendments/addenda
- Complete all required forms
- Maintain accurate commodity subscriptions
- Monitor opportunities daily
